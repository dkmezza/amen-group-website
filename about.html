About Amen Group

Building a sustainable future through innovative agriculture and reliable logistics solutions

Who We Are

Amen Group Company Limited is a leading agricultural trading and logistics company specializing in connecting Tanzanian farmers to international markets. We are the vital link between local agricultural producers and global buyers, ensuring fair prices for farmers and reliable supply for our international partners.

Our core business focuses on the procurement, processing, and export of high-quality agricultural commodities including grains (maize, rice, and sorghum), pulses (all varieties of beans and peas), and oilseeds (sesame seeds and groundnuts). We work directly with farming communities across Tanzania, providing them with access to lucrative international markets while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Through our integrated approach combining agricultural trading expertise with efficient logistics solutions, we ensure seamless movement of commodities from farm to international destination, creating value for all stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain.
