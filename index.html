About Amen Group

Amen Group Company Limited is a leading agricultural trading and logistics company connecting Tanzanian farmers to international markets. We specialize in the procurement, quality assurance, and export of high-quality agricultural commodities including grains, pulses, and oilseeds.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between agricultural production and market access, creating value for farmers, businesses, and consumers alike.
